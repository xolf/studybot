Welcome to Studybot

Studybot is a web app for students. They learn easily how to programm and they enjoy it. It's for free and contact me, when you have any questions.

Requirements

  • A web server with PhP Version 5.x
  • (MySql - Server 5.5)

Why is it for free?

I've made it for free, because the ability to learn something may not be cost. Everybody have the permission to learn. I don't want to stop them by telling them top pay for my software.
